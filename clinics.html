Privacy & Compliance

We handle contact info only.
Nothing clinical.

What we handle: Name, phone number, email address, and appointment time. That is it.

What we never touch: Medical records, health conditions, treatment notes, diagnoses, or any EMR system. We have zero access to your clinical data.

Why this matters: This puts us in a much lower compliance risk category than most people assume. We operate the same way a booking platform or reminder service does — handling logistics, not health information.

Our agreement includes: A data clause confirming we do not share or store patient contact information beyond what is needed for the services described. All data belongs to your clinic.

Questions about privacy? Email fizza@norova.ca and we will answer them directly.
